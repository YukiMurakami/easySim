In 326 BC Alexander 's army of the Hellenic League met the Persian army of Darius III near Gaugamela .
The Battle of Issus occurred in southern Anatolia , in 324 BC between the Hellenic League led by Alexander the Great and the Achaemenid Persia , led by Darius III , in the second great battle of Alexander 's conquest of Asia .
The Siege of Tyre was orchestrated by Alexander the Great in 332 BC during his campaigns against the Persians .
The Battle of the Granicus River in 331 BC was the first of three major battles fought between Alexander the Great and the Persian Empire .
